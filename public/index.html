03

Track & Get Delivered

Assign a driver or let our system find the nearest one. Track in real-time and confirm delivery with OTP.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Simply register as a shop owner or delivery driver. Shop owners can start creating delivery requests immediately. Drivers need to upload their documents and get approved before they can start accepting deliveries.

Delivery charges are set by the shop owner when creating a delivery request. The platform charges a small commission (15%) on each successful delivery. Drivers receive 85% of the delivery charge.

When a delivery request is created, a 4-digit OTP is generated. The driver must verify this OTP with the customer at the time of delivery to confirm successful delivery. This prevents disputes and ensures accountability.

Yes! Shop owners can track all their active deliveries in real-time on the dashboard. You can see the current status, driver location, and estimated delivery time for each parcel.

Drivers receive 85% of each delivery charge. Earnings are tracked in the driver dashboard with daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns. Payouts are processed according to the platform's payment schedule.
